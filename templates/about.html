ℹ️ About DualCrop

🎯 Our Mission

DualCrop Smart Advisory System is an AI-powered application designed to help farmers and agricultural professionals detect crop diseases early and prevent significant yield losses. By leveraging deep learning technology, we provide accessible and accurate disease detection for multiple crop types.

✨ Key Features

🤖
AI-Powered Detection
MobileNetV2 transfer learning model trained on thousands of leaf images
⚡
Real-Time Analysis
Get instant predictions in seconds with high accuracy
🌾
Multiple Crops
Support for brinjal, grapes, and other major crops
📱
Easy to Use
Simple drag-and-drop interface accessible on all devices

🌱 Supported Crops & Diseases

Our system can detect diseases in the following crops:

  • Brinjal: Healthy Leaf
  • Grapes: Healthy, Black Rot, Esca (Black Measles), Leaf Blight
